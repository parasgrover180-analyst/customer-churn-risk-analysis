# Customer Churn & Revenue Risk Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior and revenue risk exposure using SQL and Power BI.

The objective of this project was to:

- Identify key churn drivers  
- Segment customers based on risk level  
- Quantify revenue exposure from high-risk customers  
- Analyze cohort revenue trends  
- Provide business-focused retention insights  

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

- MySQL (Database & Data Cleaning)
- SQL (Business Logic & Analysis)
- Power BI (Data Modeling & Dashboard)
- DAX (KPI Calculations & Risk Metrics)

---

## Key Business Metrics

- Total Customers
- Total Revenue
- Churn Rate (High-Risk Proxy)
- Revenue at Risk
- Risk Segment Contribution %
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Customer Lifetime Revenue

---

## Key Insights

- High-Risk customers contribute significant revenue exposure.
- Low purchase frequency customers show higher churn probability.
- Revenue growth trend fluctuates across monthly cohorts.
- Failure severity impacts churn likelihood.

---

##  Business Recommendations

- Prioritize retention strategy for High-Risk revenue segment.
- Implement targeted offers for low-frequency customers.
- Monitor monthly revenue risk trends.
- Build proactive engagement strategy for top revenue customers.
